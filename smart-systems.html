Smart Features & Technology

Advanced lighting, monitoring, and smart home features that make this boat truly modern and connected.

  • Energy Efficient: Low power consumption for extended off-grid living

  • Touch Controls: Responsive dimming switches

Global Remote Monitoring & Custom Smart Display

A Raspberry Pi running the powerful Victron Cerbo GX software allows you to monitor the entire electrical system—from battery state of charge to solar yield—from anywhere in the world via the Victron Remote Management (VRM) online portal. Check in on your boat from your phone or laptop, wherever you are.

The custom-built smart display provides an at-a-glance command center, showing all critical data in real time: battery stats, solar input, water tank levels, indoor environment (temp/humidity), and local weather.

Advanced VRM Remote Monitoring

Monitor every aspect of your boat's systems remotely with comprehensive data and analytics.

  • GPS Tracking: Know exactly where your boat is at all times
  • Battery Monitoring: Real-time State of Charge (SoC) and temperature
  • Water Tank Levels: Never run out of water unexpectedly
  • Solar Performance: Track daily and historical solar yield
  • System Alerts: Instant notifications for any issues
  • Historical Data: Analyze usage patterns and optimize performance

Detailed System Analytics

Access detailed charts and analytics to understand your boat's performance and plan your journeys.

  • Power Consumption: Track usage patterns and optimize efficiency
  • Charging Cycles: Monitor battery health and performance
  • Environmental Data: Temperature, humidity, and weather integration
  • System Health: Proactive monitoring of all electrical components
  • Mobile Access: Full functionality from your smartphone
