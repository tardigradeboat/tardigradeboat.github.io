Technical Specifications

A comprehensive look at the high-quality systems and components that make this boat a comfortable and reliable home.

Advanced Electrical & Solar

Engineered for complete off-grid autonomy and peace of mind, the entire electrical system is built around high-end Victron components.

  • Solar Power: 2 x 540W panels (1080W total)
  • Leisure Battery: 12V 200Ah LiFePO4
  • Controller: Victron SmartSolar MPPT 12V/50A
  • Inverter/Charger: 800W Victron Multiplus
  • Alternator Charger: 12V 18A Victron smart charger
  • 12v and 24v systems: Separate 24v system to enable fast charging.
  • Monitoring: Victron SmartShunt for precise, real-time data.

Comfort & Convenience

Designed for comfortable year-round living, with reliable and high-quality amenities.

  • Heating: Squirrel Morso multi-fuel stove, professionally installed by Ship Shape Stoves.
  • Alternate Heating: Electric Diesel Heater in bedroom for those cold nights and freezing mornings.
  • Water System: 850L main tank & 60L dedicated drinking water tank with 3-stage filtration.
  • Hot Water: New Paloma gas boiler, installed by a certified specialist.
  • Waste Management: The simple and eco-friendly Simploo compost toilet.
  • Lighting & Power: Bespoke, fully dimmable LED lighting throughout, plus high-power USB-C and 240V plug sockets everywhere you need them.

Engine & Hull

The core of the boat is solid, reliable, and has been meticulously maintained.

  • Engine: The legendary Perkins 4108. A true workhorse, serviced in June 2024 and has never missed a beat.
  • Hull: Professionally surveyed and blackened in June 2024, ensuring it's protected and in excellent condition for years to come.
